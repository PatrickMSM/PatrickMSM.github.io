So what exactly is a "Browser Fingerprint"? A browser fingerprint is a unique identifier (UID) that is attached to you and your browser. Something like a unique name. You can test this out by opening a new incognito tab and noticing that the number stays the same. How does this work? It works by analyzing your screen resolution, operating system, and so and ClientJS calculates it into a number that uniquely distinguishes you.
